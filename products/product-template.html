About the X1 Pro

The SpeedMaster X1 Pro represents the pinnacle of urban electric cycling. Designed and engineered by our in-house team of mobility specialists, this bike combines cutting-edge battery technology with a beautifully crafted aluminium frame to deliver a riding experience unlike anything else in its class.

The 250W Bafang mid-drive motor provides natural, smooth assistance that responds to your pedalling cadence — making hills feel flat and long commutes effortless. The integrated 36V Samsung cell battery is hidden inside the frame for a clean, traditional bicycle silhouette, and can be removed for indoor charging.

Hydraulic disc brakes front and rear provide consistent stopping power in all weather conditions. The colour LCD display shows speed, battery level, assist mode, and total distance, with a convenient USB charging port for your phone.

  • Lightweight 6061 aluminium frame — strong, corrosion resistant
  • Shimano 8-speed gearing for versatile riding
  • Ergonomic saddle with suspension seatpost
  • Puncture-resistant 700c tyres
  • Integrated front and rear lights (USB rechargeable)
  • Compatible with all standard accessories

Full Technical Specifications

All specifications are measured under standard test conditions. Real-world range may vary based on rider weight, terrain, and assist mode selected.

  • Motor: 250W Bafang M200 mid-drive
  • Battery: 36V 14.5Ah (522Wh) Samsung 21700 cells
  • Range: Up to 50km (Eco mode) / 30km (Sport mode)
  • Charge time: 4–5 hours (standard 2A charger)
  • Top speed: 25 km/h (electronically limited, EU/UK legal)
  • Frame: 6061 T6 aluminium, hydroformed
  • Fork: Alloy rigid / optional SR Suntour upgrade
  • Brakes: Shimano MT200 hydraulic disc, 180mm rotors
  • Gears: Shimano Altus 8-speed
  • Tyres: Schwalbe Road Cruiser 700x38c
  • Display: 3.5" colour LCD with USB output
  • Weight: 22.4kg (bike) / 3.1kg (battery)
  • Max rider weight: 120kg
  • Colours: Midnight Black, Signal Yellow, Glacier White
  • Sizes available: S (155–175cm), M (170–185cm), L (180–195cm)
