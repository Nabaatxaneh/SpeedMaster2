About This Store

The Glasgow store was opened in response to the rapidly growing demand for electric mobility solutions across Scotland. Our team of Scottish-based technicians brings the same level of expertise and dedication as our English stores. We service all major makes and models and carry a full selection of spare parts for quick turnaround repairs.

Glasgow's flat city centre streets and growing network of cycle infrastructure make it an ideal city for electric scooters and e-bikes. Our staff are passionate about urban mobility and are always happy to advise on the best commuting setup for your specific routes and needs.
