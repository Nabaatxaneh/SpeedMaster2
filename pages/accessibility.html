Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 1 April 2026

SpeedMaster Ltd is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or disability. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

Our Commitment

We believe that every person should be able to access information about electric bikes and scooters, browse our product range, and complete purchases independently. Accessibility is not an afterthought — it is built into how we design and develop our digital presence.

We actively work to:

  • Ensure sufficient colour contrast between text and background elements
  • Provide text alternatives for all non-text content, including images and icons
  • Ensure all functionality is available via keyboard navigation
  • Ensure form elements have clearly associated labels
  • Structure content with meaningful headings and landmarks
  • Provide clear focus indicators for keyboard users
  • Ensure interactive elements have accessible names and roles
  • Support users who increase text size or use browser zoom
  • Respect users' preferences for reduced motion

Known Limitations

While we strive for full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, we are aware of the following limitations that we are actively working to address:

  • Some third-party embeds (including map placeholders and payment widgets) may not be fully accessible. We are working with vendors to improve this.
  • Some older PDF documents linked from our website may not be fully accessible to screen readers. We are reviewing and updating these.
  • Video content on the website currently lacks closed captions. We are working to add these to all video content.

Assistive Technology

Our website has been tested with the following assistive technologies:

  • NVDA screen reader with Firefox (Windows)
  • VoiceOver with Safari (macOS and iOS)
  • TalkBack with Chrome (Android)
  • Keyboard-only navigation (Chrome and Firefox)

Text Size and Display

You can adjust the text size using your browser's built-in zoom controls. Our website is designed to remain functional and readable at up to 200% zoom. If you use a custom stylesheet, our content should remain accessible.

Links and Navigation

All navigational elements are clearly labelled and accessible by keyboard. Links are identified with meaningful text, and focus states are visible for keyboard users. Skip-to-content links are available at the top of each page.

Reporting Accessibility Issues

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you experience any barriers to access, or if you find content that is not accessible to you, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@speedmaster.bike
  • Phone: 0121 234 5678
  • Post: SpeedMaster Ltd, 42 Corporation Street, Birmingham, B4 6SX

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and will make every effort to resolve any issues raised promptly.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response to an accessibility concern, you may contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS) at equalityadvisoryservice.com.

Technical Information

This website is built with standard HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. We do not use any technology that is known to create barriers for users of assistive technology. The website has been designed mobile-first and is responsive across all device sizes.

This statement was prepared on 1 April 2026 and will be reviewed annually, or when significant changes are made to the website.
