Products

Currently, privately-owned electric scooters are only legal to ride on private land in the UK. Rental e-scooters (part of official government trials) are legal on public roads in designated areas. However, legislation is expected to change. In the meantime, all e-scooters we sell are road-legal once legislation changes, and can be used on private land now. We actively support the campaign to legalise privately-owned e-scooters.
An e-bike is a bicycle with an integrated electric motor that assists your pedalling (pedelec). E-bikes are fully legal on UK public roads and cycle paths, subject to a maximum assisted speed of 25 km/h and a motor output of 250W. An e-scooter is a stand-on scooter with an electric motor — the rider stands on a platform rather than sitting or pedalling. Currently only legal on private land in the UK (see above).
Range varies significantly depending on the battery capacity, your weight, terrain, wind conditions, and the level of assistance selected. As a general guide, our e-bikes offer 30–100km per charge. E-scooters typically offer 25–70km. These figures are based on real-world testing. All product pages show the expected range under our standard test conditions.

Delivery

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (next working day) is available on most products. Click & Collect from any of our stores is typically ready within 48 hours. All bikes are delivered fully assembled by our two-man delivery team.
Free standard delivery is available on all orders over £500. For orders under £500, standard delivery is £29.99. Express delivery is £49.99 regardless of order value. Click & Collect is always free.

Returns

We offer a 30-day no-quibble returns policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it in its original packaging for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in unused condition — if a bike or scooter has been ridden, a 15% restocking fee may apply. To start a return, email returns@speedmaster.bike.
In the unlikely event your order arrives damaged or with a fault, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos and your order number. We'll arrange a free collection and either a replacement or full refund — whichever you prefer. All our products carry a minimum 12-month warranty against manufacturing defects.

Repairs

Booking in advance is recommended for all repair work. You can book online via our Repairs page or call your nearest store. We do accept walk-ins for minor repairs and quick fixes, but waiting times may be longer without a booking.

Finance

We offer Klarna financing (Pay in 3, Pay in 30 days, and monthly instalments), as well as traditional credit finance on orders over £299. 0% APR promotional offers are available on selected models. Visit our Finance page for full details and representative examples.
