About This Store

Edinburgh has embraced electric mobility enthusiastically, and our Princes Street store is right at the heart of it. The store occupies a bright, modern space on the ground floor of a Grade A listed building and features an impressive display of our full range, from entry-level scooters to top-of-the-range performance e-bikes.

Our Edinburgh workshop team is trained to service and repair all major brands and is particularly experienced with the unique demands of Scottish cycling — from cobbled Old Town streets to hilly terrain. We recommend our Full Service package for any bike used regularly on Edinburgh's challenging topography.

Extended opening hours on Sundays make us especially convenient for weekend visitors to the city and those who find it difficult to visit during the working week.
