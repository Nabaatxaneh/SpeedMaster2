SpeedMaster Manchester

Located on one of Manchester's most iconic streets, our Deansgate store brings the full SpeedMaster experience to the North West. Situated in a stunning converted Victorian building, the store features a bright, spacious showroom with the complete range of electric bikes and scooters, as well as a fully-equipped repair workshop at the rear.

About This Store

Our Manchester store serves the Greater Manchester area and the wider North West region. We carry the full SpeedMaster range alongside official parts and accessories, and our technicians are trained on all major brands. The Deansgate location was chosen for its excellent transport links and is easily reachable from across the city.

Test rides are available on a wide selection of models — please contact the store in advance to book your slot. We can also arrange home delivery of your purchase from the store, or Click & Collect for online orders.

Getting Here

  • Tram: Metrolink — Deansgate–Castlefield stop (2 min walk)
  • Train: Manchester Oxford Road (8 min walk) or Manchester Piccadilly (15 min walk)
  • Bus: Numerous services via Deansgate
  • Parking: Great Northern car park (3 min walk)
