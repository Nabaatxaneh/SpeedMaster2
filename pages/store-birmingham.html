SpeedMaster Birmingham

Our Birmingham store is our original flagship location, situated in the heart of the city centre on Corporation Street. It's our largest store, featuring a full showroom floor with over 50 models on display, a dedicated test ride area, and our busiest repair workshop.

About This Store

The Birmingham store offers the full SpeedMaster experience. Whether you're looking to purchase your first e-scooter, upgrade your current ride, or bring a vehicle in for a service, our team of experts is here to help. We stock the complete range of e-bikes and e-scooters from all major brands, plus a comprehensive selection of accessories, spare parts, and safety equipment.

Our repair workshop operates Monday to Saturday and offers same-day service for many common repairs. We're an official service centre for AOVO, VIPCOO, KUGOO, TOMINI, PURE, and KAABO, and we service all other brands too.

Free customer parking is available on Steelhouse Lane (5 minutes walk). The store is also easily accessible by tram from Birmingham New Street (Corporation Street stop) and is a short walk from the city's main bus routes on Bull Street.

Getting Here

  • Tram: West Midlands Metro — Corporation Street stop
  • Train: Birmingham New Street (10 min walk) or Snow Hill (5 min walk)
  • Bus: Multiple routes stop on Corporation Street
  • Parking: Steelhouse Lane car park (5 min walk)
