Repairs & Servicing

SpeedMaster is the UK's official repair centre and authorised retailer for the country's leading electric scooter and e-bike brands, including AOVO, VIPCOO, KUGOO, TOMINI, PURE, and KAABO. Our certified technicians service all major makes and models — whether you bought from us or not.

With four fully-equipped workshops across Birmingham, Manchester, Glasgow, and Edinburgh, we offer fast turnaround, fair transparent pricing, and a 90-day repair guarantee on all work carried out.

Repair Services

Our workshops are staffed by manufacturer-trained technicians who can diagnose and repair faults on virtually any electric scooter or e-bike on the market. We use only genuine OEM parts and premium aftermarket alternatives where specified.

What We Fix

  • Battery diagnostics, cell balancing, and full replacements
  • Motor replacements — hub and mid-drive
  • Controller faults and replacements
  • Brake adjustments, pad replacement, and hydraulic line bleeds
  • Tyre and inner tube replacement (10", 8.5", and 700c)
  • Display unit repairs and software updates / resets
  • Throttle and brake lever replacements
  • Folding mechanism adjustment and repair
  • Suspension servicing (fork and rear shock)
  • Full strip-down and deep clean service
  • Annual health check and safety inspection
  • Water damage assessment and treatment

Repair Warranty

We stand behind the quality of our workmanship. All repairs carried out by SpeedMaster technicians come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labour. If the same fault recurs within 90 days of your repair, we'll fix it at no additional charge.

This warranty is in addition to any statutory rights you may have under UK consumer law. It applies to the specific repair work carried out and does not cover unrelated faults, accidental damage, or misuse.
