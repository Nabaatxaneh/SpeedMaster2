About SpeedMaster

SpeedMaster was founded in Birmingham in 2019 with a simple but ambitious goal: to make premium electric mobility accessible to everyone in the UK. What started as a single shop on Corporation Street has grown into a national network of four stores, an official repair centre, and an online presence serving thousands of customers across the country.

Our Story

The story of SpeedMaster begins with our founder, a lifelong cycling enthusiast who became frustrated by the lack of expert, trustworthy advice available to consumers navigating the rapidly expanding e-scooter and e-bike market. In 2019, he opened the Birmingham store with a team of three, determined to do things differently — to offer genuine expert knowledge, transparent pricing, and a repair service that actually worked.

Word spread quickly. Within 18 months, the Birmingham store was the highest-rated electric vehicle retailer in the Midlands. Manufacturer partnerships followed — first AOVO, then VIPCOO, then KUGOO and beyond — transforming SpeedMaster into the UK's official authorised retailer and repair centre for the country's leading electric mobility brands.

The Manchester store opened in 2021. Glasgow followed in 2022, and Edinburgh in 2024. Today, SpeedMaster has a team of over 60 dedicated specialists, has serviced more than 12,000 vehicles, and has helped tens of thousands of customers find their perfect ride.

We remain proudly independent. We don't answer to shareholders or private equity. We answer to our customers — and that's exactly how we like it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to accelerate the adoption of clean, electric urban transport across the UK by providing the best possible product range, the most knowledgeable staff, and the most reliable after-sales support in the industry.

We believe that electric bikes and scooters are more than just consumer products — they're a meaningful contribution to cleaner cities, reduced congestion, and a more sustainable future. Every vehicle we sell is one fewer car journey. Every repair we complete extends the life of a vehicle that might otherwise end up in landfill. We take that responsibility seriously.

Our Team

Behind every SpeedMaster interaction is a team of passionate, knowledgeable people who genuinely love what they do. Our floor staff are all experienced riders who can talk knowledgeably about range, motor types, battery chemistry, and legal regulations. Our technicians are manufacturer-trained and continuously upskilled as new models and technologies arrive.

We invest heavily in our people. Every member of the SpeedMaster team receives ongoing training, and we actively promote from within. Our Birmingham workshop manager started as a sales assistant in 2020. Our Head of Repairs joined as a junior technician in 2021. We're a company built on expertise, and we're proud of the careers we've built.

Our team currently includes:

  • 18 certified repair technicians across 4 workshops
  • 20+ product specialists and sales advisors
  • A dedicated customer service team available Mon–Sat
  • In-house logistics and delivery coordination
  • A digital marketing team based at Birmingham HQ

Our Values

Everything at SpeedMaster flows from four core values that we return to when making every decision — from how we hire to how we price to how we handle a complaint.

  • Expertise: We know our products inside out and share that knowledge freely. We never oversell, and we always recommend the right product for your actual needs.
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no confusing terms, no misleading claims. Honest pricing, honest advice, honest repairs.
  • Community: We're rooted in the cities we serve. We support local events, partner with cycling clubs and commuter groups, and actively campaign for better cycling infrastructure and sensible e-scooter legislation.
  • Sustainability: We take our environmental responsibilities seriously. We use recycled packaging, responsibly dispose of old batteries, and actively reduce energy use across our stores and workshops.
