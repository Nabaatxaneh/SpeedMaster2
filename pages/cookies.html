Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how SpeedMaster Ltd ("SpeedMaster", "we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at speedmaster.bike. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to the website owner. Cookies allow a website to recognise your device and remember preferences and settings between visits.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable core functionality such as session management, shopping basket, and security features. These cannot be disabled as the website cannot function without them.

  • session_id — Maintains your session across pages. Duration: session.
  • cart_token — Stores your shopping cart contents. Duration: 30 days.
  • csrf_token — Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery. Duration: session.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance and usability.

  • _ga — Google Analytics. Tracks user sessions and behaviour. Duration: 2 years.
  • _gid — Google Analytics. Distinguishes users. Duration: 24 hours.
  • _gat — Google Analytics. Throttles request rate. Duration: 1 minute.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred currency, stored addresses, or recently viewed items) and provide enhanced, more personalised features.

  • pref_currency — Stores your preferred currency. Duration: 1 year.
  • recently_viewed — Remembers recently viewed products. Duration: 30 days.
  • cookie_consent — Stores your cookie consent preferences. Duration: 1 year.

Marketing and Targeting Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They also track whether you have clicked on an ad and help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

  • _fbp — Facebook Pixel. Used to deliver advertising. Duration: 90 days.
  • _ttp — TikTok Pixel. Advertising measurement. Duration: 13 months.
  • IDE — Google DoubleClick. Advertising measurement. Duration: 1 year.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third parties, including Google, Meta (Facebook), TikTok, Klarna, and Trustpilot. These third parties set their own cookies and have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to read. We do not control these cookies.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

  • Our cookie banner: When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie consent banner. You can choose which categories of cookies to accept.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality. For guidance, visit your browser's help pages.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: You can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. The "last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
